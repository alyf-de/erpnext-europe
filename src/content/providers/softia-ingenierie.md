### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

France (primary market)

## ERPNext Experience

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

CRM, Sales, Production, Finance, HR (based on Functional Consultant certification scope)

## Technical Capabilities

### What is your customization philosophy?

Low-code solutions; develops custom applications using Frappe framework; emphasis on tailor-made information systems and business process optimization

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes. Frappe Framework Developer certified; develops custom Low Code applications directly in ERPNext environment

## Project Approach

### What does your typical implementation process look like?

- Design and implementation of information systems
- End-to-end deployment with customized, high-performance, scalable architecture
- Focus on maximizing efficiency and competitiveness
- Integration of AI and custom development for system strengthening

Yes (pre-sales, implementation, customization, migration, and training offered)
