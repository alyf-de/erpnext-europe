## Company Overview

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

Spain (Galicia region)

## ERPNext Experience

### Since when have you been implementing ERPNext?

ct.galega has 20 years of experience implementing enterprise management systems (founded 2005 with a prior decade of expertise). They use Frappe technologies internally (a Frappe Drive instance is hosted at tenda.ctgalega.com). They participate in Spain's Kit Digital program, co-financed by European Next Generation EU funds, to help companies in Galicia access subsidies for implementing digital solutions.

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Business management and ERP systems. Specialized solutions for installation and maintenance companies as well as distribution companies.

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you typically implement?

Through their Baseges ERP/CRM/BI application, ct.galega covers: CRM (customer relationship management, budgets, opportunities, commercial actions, reports, alerts, KPIs, document management), sales (budgets and offers, customer orders), purchasing (supply management, purchase traceability), inventory and stock (normal, special, reserved, free and predictable stock, batch management, serial numbers, warranties, expiration dates, location tracking, multi-code system, barcode management), logistics and picking, invoicing, financial management and accounting, and business intelligence.

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Spanish business management compliance through their Baseges application. GDPR audit services offered as part of their data protection and information security services.

## Technical Capabilities

### What is your customization philosophy?

Tailored solutions to meet specific client needs. When standard software does not meet a company's specific needs, ct.galega develops custom applications in various environments and technologies, including web applications, desktop applications, and mobile apps.

### Which hosting options do you offer or support?

IT infrastructure services including system maintenance and support. Frappe-based tools deployed internally (Frappe Drive instance at tenda.ctgalega.com).

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

- Microsoft partnerships
- 3CX integration
- DocShare integration
- BitDefender integration

Consulting, implementation, training, and support services

## Post-Go-Live Support

Yes, IT system maintenance and support

Training services included

Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

20+ years of ERP system experience built on a decade of previous expertise before founding in 2005.
