## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

CoreBiz Works covers the typical business process modules including:
- CRM (lead management, customer relationships, sales opportunities)
- Sales (Vertrieb) — quotations through to invoicing
- Purchasing (Einkauf) — requisitions, purchase orders, goods receipts, supplier evaluation
- Warehouse / Inventory (Lager)
- Project Management (Projekte)
- Service / Support
- Accounting and Controlling (with German-specific reports)
- HR and Payroll (Personalwesen und Lohnabrechnung)

ERPNext modules can also be deployed individually — for example, CRM as a complement to an existing ERP system.

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes. CoreBiz Engineering specializes in:
- German accounting compliance (SKR03 implementation)
- German accounting reports (erpnext_german_accounting app)
- Adaptation of ERPNext for German market requirements
- SKR03 (standard German chart of accounts) integration for medium-sized enterprises

## Your Differentiator

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

CoreBiz Engineering's focus is on providing ERPNext adapted specifically for the German market (CoreBiz Works). Their key differentiator is:

1. **German Market Specialization**: Dedicated adaptation of ERPNext for German accounting standards (SKR03) and regulatory requirements
2. **German Accounting Reports**: Development and maintenance of erpnext_german_accounting app for German accounting compliance
3. **Flexible Infrastructure**: CoreBiz cluster approach allowing flexible deployment with multiple environments (production, staging, legacy system coexistence)
4. **Medium-Market Focus**: Tailored solutions for German Mittelstand (medium-sized enterprises)
5. **Open Source Commitment**: Active development of public Frappe apps for German accounting and business requirements
