## Functional Coverage

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes. ALYF provides:
- DATEV integration for exporting bookings to tax advisors and DATEV Unternehmen Online
- German accounting reports via erpnext_germany app
- EU VAT ID validation (automatic every 3 months or on demand)
- SKR03/SKR04 standard German chart of accounts support
- E-invoice integration (eu_einvoice app for creating and importing e-invoices)
- Business trip management (Dienstreise)

## Your Differentiator

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

ALYF's primary differentiator is their deep technical contribution to the ERPNext and Frappe ecosystem. With over 100,000 lines of code contributed—more than any other service provider worldwide—they combine implementation expertise with core platform development. Leadership (particularly Raffael Meyer) is deeply involved in ERPNext security, translations, and German tax compliance features. Their focus on German regulatory compliance through the erpnext_germany app, DATEV integration, and open-source contributions makes them a unique combination of implementers and core platform maintainers.
