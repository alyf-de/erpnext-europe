## Company Overview

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

Spain (particularly Galicia), with offices in Vigo and A Coruña

## ERPNext Experience

### Since when have you been implementing ERPNext?

Sinerkia is the first official ERPNext service provider in Spain. The company was founded in 2015 and has extensive experience with multiple ERP systems (Odoo, Openbravo, Dolibarr) before adding ERPNext. The exact date they began ERPNext implementations is not publicly stated.

### Do you contribute to ERPNext core or maintain publicly available Frappe apps? If yes, please link them.

Maintains Odoo modules in Odoo App Store (over 110 Odoo modules developed with 800,000+ lines of code). No public ERPNext core contributions or Frappe apps were identified as of this research.

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Healthcare (Medical Sinerkia solution for clinical management)

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

CRM, Sales, Purchasing, Invoicing, Inventory/Warehouse, Manufacturing/Production, Accounting, Project Management. Sinerkia adapts ERPNext to all types of businesses: retail, factories, warehouses and services.

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Spain requires compliance with the SII (Suministro Inmediato de Informacion) system for VAT reporting to the AEAT, and the upcoming VeriFactu regulation (mandatory from January 2027 for corporate taxpayers) for verified electronic invoicing with SHA-256 hash chains and QR codes. Sinerkia adapts ERPNext to Spanish business needs. Specific SII/VeriFactu integration details should be confirmed directly with Sinerkia.
