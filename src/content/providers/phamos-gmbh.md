## Your Differentiator

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

phamos GmbH's key differentiators include:

1. **100% ERPNext Focus**: Company is entirely dedicated to ERPNext implementations—no other products or services, allowing deep specialization

2. **Spin-Off Origin**: Created as a dedicated ERPNext company by spinning off the ERPNext department from tüit GmbH, combining ERPNext heritage with focused expertise

3. **Module-by-Module Implementation Philosophy**: Gradual, step-by-step approach that introduces ERPNext into customer operations module by module, prioritizing team acceptance and process understanding

4. **Process-Centric Approach**: Works intensively with customer workflows rather than imposing standard implementations; emphasizes communication and documentation

5. **Weekly Stakeholder Engagement**: Project managers maintain weekly appointments with customer project managers to ensure alignment on requirements, timelines, and priorities

6. **Comprehensive Documentation**: Maintains extensive ERPNext documentation in German (doku.phamos.eu) including handbooks and guides

7. **German Market Expertise**: Full German regulatory compliance support (DATEV, e-invoicing, accounting)

8. **Consistent Growth**: Nearly 15 implementations completed with continuous learning from each project

9. **Certified Bronze Partner**: Official Frappe certification with Process Maturity Level 4
