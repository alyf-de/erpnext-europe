## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

- Accounting (Buchhaltung) — including Swiss-specific QR invoices, CAMT import, PAIN export, VAT declarations
- Sales / Order Management (Auftragswesen) — full customer order lifecycle from quotation creation through order confirmation to invoicing and delivery, with real-time order tracking
- Purchasing (Einkauf) — supplier and procurement management
- Warehouse / Inventory (Lager) — real-time stock management with goods receipt/issue tracking, multi-location inventory, and automated reordering
- Custom modules developed on the Frappe Framework to fit specific business processes

Yes, Swiss-specific regulatory requirements including QR invoices, CAMT/PAIN banking standards, and Swiss VAT declarations
