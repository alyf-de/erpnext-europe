## Company Overview

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

Austria, Germany, Switzerland, and across Europe

## ERPNext Experience

### Since when have you been implementing ERPNext?

fairkom has been working with open source frameworks including Frappe and ERPNext, with their own operations using ERPNext for accounting, invoicing, and double-entry bookkeeping

### Do you contribute to ERPNext core or maintain publicly available Frappe apps? If yes, please link them.

Yes. fairkom maintains several open source projects on GitHub (https://github.com/fairkom) and GitLab (https://git.fairkom.net), though specific ERPNext/Frappe contributions are primarily hosted on their GitLab infrastructure. They have published the "Austria - Chart of Accounts - Einheitskontenrahmen provided by fairkom.eu" as a certified Austrian chart of accounts for ERPNext.

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Service sector (fairkom itself operates as a service organization)

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

Accounting, invoicing, project management, time tracking, order management, double-entry bookkeeping

### For any modules you don't cover in-house, how do you handle them?

fairkom partners with external organizations (such as Alyf GmbH in Germany) for implementation support and consulting due to capacity constraints. They do not currently offer ERPNext hosting and consulting services themselves.

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

fairkom publishes certified Austrian charts of accounts (Kontorahmen Österreich) for ERPNext based on the Austrian "Einheitskontenrahmen" (unified chart of accounts). —

## Technical Capabilities

### Which hosting options do you offer or support?

fairkom offers fairweb hosting with EU-based, encrypted, climate-neutral infrastructure. They run their IT infrastructure on virtualized Linux servers in their Kubernetes cluster within the EU or on premises at customer locations or partner hosting providers. Optional full access to ISPconfig tool for managing web storage, databases, email addresses, and DNS entries.

### What is your customization philosophy?

fairkom provides "secure, license-free and future-proof software applications that can be adapted and integrated." Their approach emphasizes open source customization and integration into existing systems with recourse to proven open source modules.

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

WordPress/WooCommerce integration, Single-Sign-On, chat integration, email, newsletter services. ERPNext integration with WordPress online shop for automatic merchandise management.

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes, fairkom provides customization and programming services as part of their consulting offering.
