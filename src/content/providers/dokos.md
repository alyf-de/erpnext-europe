### How many team members work primarily on ERPNext/Frappe projects?

4

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

France, Europe (focus on European market)

## ERPNext Experience

### Since when have you been implementing ERPNext?

Since 2016 (when company was founded; Dokos is an ERPNext fork created from inception)

### Do you contribute to ERPNext core or maintain publicly available Frappe apps? If yes, please link them.

Yes. Maintains Dokos fork (https://github.com/dokos-io) and multiple apps: Mautic integration (https://github.com/dokos-io/mautic), available on GitLab (https://gitlab.com/dokos/dokos)

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Manufacturing and production-oriented businesses (based on platform marketing)

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

Accounting, billing, CRM, e-commerce, manufacturing, project management, third-places management, HR/HRMS, inventory management

### For any modules you don't cover in-house, how do you handle them?

Offers customization services; can add modules as needed

## Technical Capabilities

### Which hosting options do you offer or support?

Self-hosted (open-source, no license fees), Managed cloud hosting (Dokos Cloud at https://cloud.dokos.io)

### What is your customization philosophy?

Fully customizable low-code framework (Dodock/Frappe); emphasis on flexibility, European compliance, and avoiding vendor lock-in

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

Mautic integration; Dodock framework allows installing other applications

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes, through customization and development services

## Post-Go-Live Support

### Do you offer ongoing support contracts or managed services after go-live?

Yes (hosting services and professional support included)

### What are your support hours?

Monday to Friday

### Which training formats do you offer?

Free coaching (4 hours available), documentation, tutorial videos

## References & Track Record

### What is the typical size of your clients?

SMEs

## Pricing

### How do you structure your pricing?

Cloud hosting and services available (pricing model available at https://cloud.dokos.io/nuage/pricing); offers free software with optional paid services

## Your Differentiator

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

- 100% free and open-source (no licensing fees)
- European-focused adaptation with French and European regulatory compliance built in
- Based on Dodock (Frappe fork) for additional flexibility
- Emphasis on avoiding vendor lock-in
- Can choose own hosting provider without dependence on single integrator
- Dedicated to accelerating Frappe/ERPNext development in Europe
