## Company Overview

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

Primarily Italy (Italian language focus); Switzerland through solede.ch

## ERPNext Experience

### Do you contribute to ERPNext core or maintain publicly available Frappe apps? If yes, please link them.

Yes, maintains SoledeERP (https://www.solede.ch/) - a customized distribution of ERPNext with Italian language support and localization

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Manufacturing with emphasis on production planning and quality control

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

Multi-company and multi-currency management, General ledger and financial reporting, Purchase and vendor management, Sales and order management, Inventory management, Production planning, CRM and lead tracking, E-commerce integration

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Specialized in Italian regulatory requirements (not DACH); focus on Italian e-invoicing standards and Italian business needs

## Technical Capabilities

### Which hosting options do you offer or support?

- Managed hosting on Swiss/European servers, GDPR-compliant
- Hosting starting from EUR 50/month with base support included
- 99.9% uptime guarantee, automatic daily backups
- No license fees, no limit on number of users

### What is your customization philosophy?

Modern, user-friendly, 100% open-source business management system. Works on any device or platform (accessible from smartphones or laptops). Designed for enterprise needs while maintaining simplicity and intuitiveness.

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

E-commerce integrations, Industry-specific modules

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes, develops custom modules for various industries (manufacturing, retail, project management, e-commerce, education)

## Project Approach

### What does your typical implementation process look like?

Implementation, customization and consulting for business digitalization. Standard SME implementations target a 4-8 week go-live timeline; complex enterprises with customizations take 3-6 months.

### How do you handle data migration from legacy systems?

Solede handles data migration from existing management systems to SoledeERP, including bulk data import and export services. Migration is used as an opportunity to clean data and adapt it to the new system.

## Post-Go-Live Support

### Do you offer ongoing support contracts or managed services after go-live?

Yes. Hosting from EUR 50/month includes base support. Assistance guaranteed within 24 hours. Customizations quoted on a per-project basis with transparent pricing.

### Which training formats do you offer?

On-site or remote training adapted to the company's specific needs, with fully localized Italian documentation and interface. Training is designed to build team confidence and efficiency.

## References & Track Record

### What is the typical size of your clients?

SMEs (Small and Medium-sized Enterprises)

## Pricing

### How do you structure your pricing?

SoledeERP claims to save up to 70% on licensing costs compared to proprietary solutions; open-source base model

## Your Differentiator

### What makes your approach to ERPNext implementation different from others?

Italian-focused ERPNext distribution (SoledeERP) with native Italian language support and Italian regulatory compliance. Claims significant cost savings (up to 70%) compared to proprietary ERP solutions. Modern, user-friendly interface designed for accessibility across devices. Emphasis on industry-specific customizations for manufacturing (with intelligent planning and quality control), retail, project management, e-commerce, and education. 100% open-source commitment.
