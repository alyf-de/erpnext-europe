## Company Overview

### In which countries do you actively serve clients?

Germany, Luxembourg, Austria, Switzerland (DACH region)

## ERPNext Experience

### Since when have you been implementing ERPNext?

K&K Software AG committed to ERPNext in September 2019, initially to introduce it as an ERP/CRM system for their own company. After productive internal use from November 2019, they began offering ERPNext services professionally to customers starting in Q1 2021.

### Do you contribute to ERPNext core or maintain publicly available Frappe apps? If yes, please link them.

Yes. K&K Software AG maintains public repositories:
- GitHub: https://github.com/kksoftwareag

## Industry Focus

### Is there a specific industry where you have the deepest experience?

Public utilities (documented case: Gemeindewerke Adelsdorf - municipal utilities)

## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

K&K Software AG's standard ERPNext setup includes:
- CRM (lead management, customer relationships, sales opportunities, quotations through to invoicing)
- Sales & Purchasing (Verkauf & Einkauf)
- Inventory & Manufacturing (Lager & Fertigung)
- Accounting (Buchhaltung) — general ledger, accounts receivable/payable, cost centres, automated payment processes
- HR (Personalwesen) — personnel management, time recording, vacation planning, salary accounting

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes. K&K Software AG actively works on German regulatory compliance for ERPNext:
- **E-invoicing (eRechnung)**: Preparing ERPNext for XRechnung and ZUGFeRD format support, the two main German e-invoicing standards under CEN EN16931
- **DATEV**: Supports data export/transfer to DATEV for tax consultants; can be used alongside ERPNext for accounting
- **GoBD**: Awareness of GoBD-compliant archiving requirements for structured e-invoices (mandatory since January 2025 for B2B)

## Technical Capabilities

### Which hosting options do you offer or support?

Three hosting options for ERPNext:
- Public Cloud: Frappe Cloud on shared servers
- Private Cloud: Germany-based data center on exclusive servers
- On-Premises: Customer's own location

K&K Software has transitioned its own infrastructure to Frappe Cloud.

### What is your customization philosophy?

Agile approach with flexible response to customer requirements

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

- DATEV interface for tax consultant data transfer
- Local bank integrations
- E-invoicing formats (XRechnung, ZUGFeRD)
- Azure DevOps integration for project/story management during implementations

ERPNext's API-first architecture means every part of the system is an interface, allowing communication with any third-party system.

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes. When standard ERPNext cannot fully map a client's processes, K&K's dedicated software development team builds custom solutions (see softwareentwicklung.team).
