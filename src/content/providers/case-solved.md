## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

Various modules; specific list not publicly detailed

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes; focuses on UK compliance with multiple HMRC integrations:

- **HMRC MTD for VAT:** HMRC-recognised Making Tax Digital for VAT feature, mandated since 2019 for any UK VAT-registered entity. Includes a Tax Ledger tool for creating sectioned reports using filters to include General Ledger Entries for each of the 9 VAT Return boxes. Freely available to hosted customers, with a per-submission charge.
- **PAYE Real Time Information (RTI):** HMRC RTI module (in progress), mandated for all companies since 2013. Integrates with Employee Salary Slips and submits directly to HMRC, retrieving and displaying all RTI and Generic Notifications to staff. Will be freely available to hosted customers.
- **Company Tax & Annual Return (CT600):** CT600 feature listed among their ERPNext product offerings.
