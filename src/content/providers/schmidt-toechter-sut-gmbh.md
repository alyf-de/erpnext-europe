## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

- Accounting/Finance (Buchhaltung)
- Project Management (Projektmanagement)
- Human Resources/Payroll (Personalverwaltung/Lohn- und Gehaltsbuchhaltung)

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes. schmidt&toechter specializes in German regulatory compliance:

- **DATEV LODAS Integration**: Proprietary app that transfers payroll-relevant data from ERPNext to DATEV LODAS including:
  - Personal master data (name, date of birth, employment date, tax ID)
  - Time data (contract data, monthly total salary)
  - Individually configured payroll components in DATEV LODAS format
  - Multi-company export support
  - Export completed in less than one hour to become operational
  - Data processing within customer's ERPNext instance (no third-party transfer)

- **German Payroll**: Comprehensive payroll management with HR module covering German-specific requirements

- **German Accounting**: Accounting compliance for German companies

- **Data Security**: Processing occurs exclusively within customer's ERPNext instance according to DSGVO (German data protection) requirements
