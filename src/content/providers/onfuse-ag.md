## Technical Capabilities

### What is your customization philosophy?

Open-source first approach; develops freely accessible apps with optional voluntary contributions

### Which third-party integrations have you built or successfully deployed?

- **PaymentAutomation**: Automated payment workflows for Swiss standards
- **camt.054 import**: Receiving and reconciling payments via camt.054 bank statement files, automating payment matching
- **QR-IBAN integration**: Configuration of QR-IBANs linked to bank accounts for automatic reconciliation using creditor references
- **Swiss Payroll Hours export**: TimeClock feeds data to ERPNext Payroll and Swiss Accounting
- **Planner**: Gantt chart project planning integrated with ERPNext Tasks
- **Insider**: Mobile app integration for ERPNext task management and employee features

All integrations are built for ERPNext v15 and v16 compatibility.

### Do you develop custom Frappe apps for clients?

Yes, develops both public and specialized ERPNext extensions
