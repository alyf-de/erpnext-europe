## Functional Coverage

### Which ERPNext modules do you implement in-house?

All standard ERPNext modules available; no implementation costs due to open-source philosophy

### For any modules you don't cover in-house, how do you handle them?

All ERPNext modules are freely available through open-source; company assists with implementation across all areas

### Do you cover local regulatory requirements (e.g. tax compliance, e-invoicing, payroll)?

Yes, extensive coverage:
- ZUGFeRD e-invoicing with PDF/A-3 format (automatically included in all invoices)
- GoBD compliance (Grundsätze zur ordnungsmäßigen Führung und Aufbewahrung von Büchern, Aufzeichnungen und Unterlagen in elektronischer Form)
- DATEV integration support
- Swiss-specific features through ERPNextSwiss
- Austrian compliance through ERPNextAustria
